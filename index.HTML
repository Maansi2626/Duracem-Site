What is GGBS?

Ground Granulated Blast Furnace Slag (GGBS) is a high-quality supplementary cementitious material made from iron blast furnace slag, a by-product of steel manufacturing. When combined with Portland cement, it enhances strength and durability.

It significantly reduces the embodied CO₂ in concrete production. GGBS concrete sets slower but gains higher strength over time and shows better resistance to chemicals, moisture, and thermal stresses.

Key Benefits

  • Reduces embodied CO₂ by up to 95%
  • Minimizes thermal cracking due to low heat of hydration
  • High resistance to aggressive chemicals (sulphates & chlorides)
  • Improves long-term compressive strength
  • Creates lighter and smoother concrete surface finishes
  • Improves workability and consistency in mixes
  • Contributes to LEED and green building certifications

Applications

Duracem GGBS is a versatile product suitable for a wide range of construction applications:

  • Mass concrete for dams, tunnels, and substructures
  • Marine and coastal structures like jetties and ports
  • Bridge decks, foundations, basements, and retaining walls
  • Urban infrastructure: roads, footpaths, stormwater drains
  • Structures exposed to harsh chemical environments
